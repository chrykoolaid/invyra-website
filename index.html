Inventory operating pillars

Built around the work that controls stock.

Invyra focuses first on the everyday inventory workflows that businesses need to trust before expanding into the wider ecosystem.

Stock Visibility

Know what is happening

Track stock levels, item details, movement history, setup gaps, and operational exceptions with clearer visibility.

Reordering

Reduce manual guesswork

Review low stock, coverage risk, supplier context, and guided reorder needs before shortages disrupt operations.

Receiving

Control stock arrival

Support full, partial, short, over, damaged, and disputed receiving with order and supplier context.

Suppliers

Connect stock to suppliers

Manage supplier records, item relationships, order history, lead times, and future supplier performance visibility.

Wastage / Store Use

Separate loss from internal use

Record waste separately from legitimate business consumption so shrinkage and store-use patterns are visible.

Scanner Ready

Prepare barcode-led workflows

Shape receiving, wastage, stocktake, store use, and movement confirmation around scanner-ready operations.

Training and trust

Train before touching live stock.

Invyra separates LIVE, TRAINING, and TEST activity so teams can learn, validate, and practise without polluting real inventory data.
