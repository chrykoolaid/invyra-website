Inventory Starter

For single-location businesses starting controlled inventory operations.

Includes
Items, suppliers, movements, basic receiving, wastage, store use, low-stock visibility, training mode, basic reports.

Inventory Professional

Purchasing and receiving control

For businesses with stronger ordering, receiving, and review workflows.

Includes
Purchase orders, reorder review, gap scan, partial/short/over receiving, damaged goods, scanner-ready workflows, approvals, expanded reports.
Best for
Retail, hospitality stockrooms, laundry/service operations, and supplier-heavy workflows.

For larger operators needing permissions, audit, device controls, and implementation support.

Includes
Multi-location foundations, advanced permissions, environment separation, audit exports, stocktake readiness, supplier performance visibility, priority support.
Best for
Multi-location businesses, audit-sensitive organisations, and controlled stock operations.
