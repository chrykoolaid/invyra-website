What it does

Control the full stock lifecycle.

Invyra Inventory is designed to help teams control stock from item setup through supplier ordering, receiving, internal use, wastage, counts, reporting, and operational review.

Dashboard

Operational visibility

Low stock, pending orders, receiving due, setup gaps, waste flags, stock value, and inventory exceptions.

Item Master

Clean item truth

Items, variants, categories, units, barcodes, supplier item codes, cost foundations, and reorder settings.

Movement Ledger

Stock history

Receipts, adjustments, transfers, waste, store use, corrections, stocktake variance, and audit trail.

Suppliers

Supplier control

Supplier records, item relationships, order history, lead times, and future performance visibility.

Orders / Receiving

Purchase flow

Draft orders, supplier orders, order amendments, partial receiving, short receiving, over receiving, and damaged goods handling.

Reorder Review

Guided replenishment

Low-stock review, coverage risk, reorder needs, explainable recommendations, and manager review.

Wastage / Store Use

Loss visibility

Separate true waste from legitimate internal business use with reason codes and cost impact.

Scanner Ready

Barcode workflows

USB and Bluetooth scanner readiness for receiving, wastage, store use, stocktake, and movement confirmation.

Training mode

Practise safely before going live.

LIVE, TRAINING, and TEST separation helps teams validate workflows without corrupting real operational stock records.

  • Staff can learn item lookup and scanner workflows.
  • Supervisors can practise receiving review and movement checks.
  • Managers can review reports, approvals, exceptions, and audit history.
  • Admins can validate users, permissions, environments, and settings.
